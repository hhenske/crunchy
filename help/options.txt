Help Topic: Options

   crunchy.py [<options>] [...]

   -h [<topic>] | --help [<topic>]

      See the 'usage' topic for help topics.

   -is | --ignore-stop

      Continue reading data and ignore the &stop directive.

   -isr | --ignore-stop-reset

      Same as above, but also reset all running values.

   -up <plugin> | --use-plugin <plugin>

      Load a plugin.  (See &help plugins.)

   -vv

      Show more verbose information.
